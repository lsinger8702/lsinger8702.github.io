Reading Questions 1b

Problem 1.

How many ways can you choose 4 kittens from a litter of 9?

Answer

$_9 C_4 = \frac{9!}{4!5!} = 126 $

Problem 2.

How many sequences of 8 nucleotides can be made using any of the 4 nucleotides A, C, G, T at each place of the sequence?

Answer

$4^8 = 65536$

Problem 3.

Suppose a sequence of 8 nucleotides contains 2 each of A, C, G, T.

How many such sequences are there?

Answer
