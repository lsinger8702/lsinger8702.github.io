听力训练（初期）Day 8

Posted on 2018-08-06 In TOEFL

TPO 8 Conversation 2

  1. Well, I know we have a test coming up on chapters, um…

    学生：我知道咱们将有次考试，是关于第几章来着？

  1. Well, I didn’t get something you said in class Monday.

    周一课堂上讲的知识我有些地方不是很明白。

    中文不懂

  2. Yeah, you were talking about a gym… a health club where people can go to exercise, that kind of thing.

    太快了

  3. Well, I was talking about an issue in strategic marketing, the health club model; I mean with a health club you might think they would have trouble attracting customers, right?

  4. Well, I know when I pass by a health club and I see all those people working out.

    working out 意思

  5. I just as soon walk on by.

    但是我不会驻足。

  6. I guess that is what I didn’t understand.

    学生：这个我就不明白了。

    太快

  7. That means better exercise equipment, high-end stuff, and classes… exercise classes, maybe aerobics.

    这就涉及了更好的健身器材、更高素质的员工以及一些健身课程，如有氧健身操。

    high-end stuff， aerobics 不认识

  8. I am not sure if I…ok I get it. Yeah.

学生：我不太确定……好了，我明白了。

似乎是连读了

  1. And also they sell an image about people having more fun, relating better to others and improving their own lives if they become members.

    反正，没听出来

  2. Pro: Well, then, can you think of another business or organization that could benefit from doing this?

    太快

  3. You mean like an administrative building?

    学生：你是指行政大楼？

    administrative 不认识

  4. Imagine public libraries; they’re an information resource for the whole community, right?

    想想公共图书馆，能为整个社区提供信息来源对吗？

    community 的 co 发音太轻，没听出来

  5. Well, like you said, better equipment, maybe a super-fast internet connection, and not just a good variety of books, but also like nice comfortable areas where people can read and do research.

    variety 太快听不清

  6. Things that make them want to come to the library and stay.

    一切能让人们多来图书馆并在此停留的方法。

    反正就是没听清。。。

总结

问题

  1. 太快听不清楚
  2. 有些特定情境下常用的词汇，或者口语常用的说法不熟悉
  3. 连读

解决办法

  1. 多练
  2. 总结记录下来，看下听力特定场景词汇总结
  3. 多练

感觉这次的录音特别清楚，所以听懂的还蛮多
