听力训练（初期）Day 9

Posted on 2018-08-07 | In TOEFL | Visitors:

TPO 9 Conversation 2

  1. What do you need?

    太快听不清

  1. Stu: Well, I received a letter in my mailbox saying that I’m supposed to return a book that I checked out back in January.

    学生:嗯,我已经邮箱里面收到一封信,上面说我应该一月份就要把书拿回去还。

    checked out 意思

  2. But because I’m writing my senior thesis, I’m supposed to be able to keep the book all semester.

    但由于我一直在写毕业论文，我本该整个学期都用到这本书的。

    senior thesis 意思

  3. It’s been recalled.

    这本书已经收回了。

  4. Do you need all of it or is there a certain section or chapter you’re working with?

    管理员：你是整本书都需要还是只需要看里面的某个部分或章节呢？

    连读

  5. Why don’t you do that for the chapter you’re working on right now?

    为什么不现在就拿这本书去影印下你需要的章节呢？

    太快

  6. We can even do the photocopying for you because of the circumstances.

    根据情况我们可以甚至可以帮你完成影印的工作。

    太快

  7. In fact, every semester we get a few students who have their borrowing privileges suspended completely because they haven’t returned books.

    事实上，每个学期我们都会遇到一些学生的延期还书期限用尽了的，就是因为他们没有把书给还了。

    suspended 意思

  8. I guess I should head back down to the dorm right now then.

    我想我现在必须马上回一趟宿舍。

  9. You don’t want to waste any time getting it back.

    你也不想把时间浪费在重新借书上吧。

  10. Now I don’t feel quite so bad about having to return the book.

    现在我也没那么在意不得不把书还了的事了。

词汇
