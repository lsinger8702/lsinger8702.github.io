听力训练（初期）Day 5

Posted on 2018-08-01 | In TOEFL | Visitors:

TPO 6 Conversation 2

  1. Well, I’ve been thinking about, you know, what you were saying in class last week, about how we shouldn’t wait until the last minute to find an idea and get started working on our term paper.

    ve been 完全听不出，until the 比较快而且不熟悉，也没听出来

  1. Well, yeah, sort of.

    sort of 有没听出来，不够熟悉

  2. See, I’ve never had a linguistics class before, so I was sort of, I mean, I was looking over the course description and a lot of the stuff you described there.

    linguistics 不认识，stuff you 连读了

  3. But there was one thing that really did jump out at me.

    did jump out at 不认识

  4. The section on dialects, ‘cause…like, that’s the kind of thing that’s always sort of intrigued me, you know?

    方言的那个章节，因为方言之类的事儿总是能吸引我的兴趣。

    dialects , intrigued 不认识；of thing 连读

  5. But you mean I realize, I mean, the scope…

    But you may not realize, I mean, the scope…

    但你可能没注意到，我是指，它的范围…

    不熟悉

  6. Well, a special now, ‘cause I’ve got like one roommate who is from the and the other one from Iraq.

    Well, especially now, ‘cause I’ve got like one roommate who is from the south and another one from New York.

    。。。不知道自己当时怎么听成这样的

  7. But then I was noticing, like, we don’t really get into this till the end of the semester, you know. So I…

    但是我注意到，直到学期末我们才真正习惯，您知道吧，所以我…

    太快，听不出来

  8. So, you want some pointers where to go for information on the subject?

    所以，你想得到些指示告诉你上哪儿找关于这个科目的相关信息？

    有点快

  9. Well, you could always start by reading the chapter in the book on sociolinguistics.

    嗯，你可以从看社会语言学书里的方言这一章节开始着手

    sociolinguistics 不认识

  10. That will give you a basic understanding of the key issues involved here.

    这样你就能对有关的重点问题有一个基本了解。

    不熟悉

  11. You know, the idea that people tend to adapt their speaking to make it closer to the speech of whomever they’re talking to, and I’m thinking, yeah, I do that when I talk with my roommates, and without even thinking about it or anything, you know.

    您知道的，就是关于人们在聊天时，会刻意调节他们的口音，使得他们的口音更接近听话者。我在想，我的确在跟我室友聊天时是这样的，也没有多想。

    太长了。。

  12. Dialect accommodation is a more manageable sort of topic.

    方言顺应是一个更容易控制的话题

    比较快，manageable 不了解

  13. Tell you what, Lisa, write me up a short proposal for this project – how you’re going to carry out the experiment and everything – a design plan.

    write me up , proposal 不了解；the experiment 不熟悉

总结

问题

  1. 不熟悉的词汇
  2. 太快，听不清，记不住
  3. 连读

解决办法

  1. 总结记录下来
  2. 多练
  3. 多练
