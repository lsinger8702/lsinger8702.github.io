听力训练（初期）Day 7

Posted on 2018-08-03 In TOEFL

##TPO 10 Conversation 1

  1. Professor Johnson, there is something that’s been on my mind.

    杰森教授，我一直在想一件事。

    been on 这种老是听不出来

  1. Remember last week, you told us that it’s really important to get our photography into a show, basically as soon as we can?

    没听出来

  2. Yup, it’s a big step, no question.

    是的。这是很重要的一步，毫无疑问。

    big step 不熟悉这种表达

  3. Thing is, I am sitting here and I am just not sure how I’d get there.

    问题是，我就坐在这里想，真的不知道怎么才能达到那一步。

    不熟悉这种表达

  4. I mean I’ve got some work I like, but is it really what a gallery is looking for?

    我倒是有些自己喜欢的作品，但是美术馆也会喜欢吗？

    没听出来

  5. How do I make the right contacts to get it into a show, I just really don’t…

    如何将自己的作品展览出来呢，我真的……

  6. OK, the first thing you should do is you absolutely have to stay true to your artistic vision; take the pictures you want to take.

    那么首先你需要做的就是坚定自己的艺术视觉，拍摄自己喜欢的照片。

  7. Don’t start trying to catch the flavor of the month and be trendy because you think you’ll get into a show – that never works, because you wind up creating something you don’t really believe in.

    千万不要因为你想展出自己的作品就试图去拍摄季风之类的时尚作品，这只会事倍功半，因为你想制作出自己其实并不热衷的作品。

    catch the flavor of the month and be trendy，**wind up ** 意思

  8. It’s uninspired, and won’t make any shows.

    听起来好像没什么灵感，也不会有成功的展出。

  9. This doesn’t mean that you should go into a cave.

    这倒不是说你应该进洞了去拍照什么的。

    听懂了但不知道是什么鬼

  10. Keep up with trends, even think about how your work might fit in with them, but don’t mindlessly follow them.

    要随着潮流走，想想你的作品如何与潮流相呼应，但是不能盲目追求。

    mindlessly 不认识

  11. I think though I have always been able to stay pretty true to what I want to create, not what others want me to create.

    我想尽管我一直坚定自己的风格，但是也许这不是别人想看到的作品啊

    理解错了意思

  12. The only thing at stake is your grade.

    只不过在教室里你还需要担心自己的分数，

    at stake 不认识

  13. I’m not talking about technique or things like that.

    technique or 连读

  14. It’s just that there is so much more at stake when you are out there making art for a living.

    就是说在你用艺术来谋生的时候，你要面临很多问题。

  15. There’s a lot of pressure to become something you are not, and people often surrender to that pressure.

    总是有很多的压力，让你不再是你自己，很多人经常在这种压力下屈服。

  16. Professor: Well, you need to be a bit of an opportunist.

    教授：呃，你需要遇到机遇。

  17. You know, common sense things like always having a sample of your work on hand to give to people.

    那么一般是你随时有张副本在手中以给人展示。

    common sense 不认识 a sample of your work 不知道是什么

  18. You won’t believe the kind of contacts and opportunities you can get in this way.

    你简直不能相信这种方式能给你带来的机遇。

  19. You’d be surprised how word gets around about photography in places like that.

    你会惊奇的发现在这些地方很多人会围观摄影作品。

    how word gets around about 不知道意思

总结

问题

  1. 换了一个题材的 conversation，好多都听不清
  2. 不熟悉的词汇

解决办法

  1. 多练
  2. 总结记录下来
