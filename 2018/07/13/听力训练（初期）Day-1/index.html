听力训练（初期）Day 1

Posted on 2018-07-13 | In TOEFL | Visitors:

TPO 2 Conversation 1

  1. You were looking at variations in climate in the Grant City area, right ?

    looking at 连读，0.8x 也完全听不出来

  2. How far along be gotten ？

    How far along have you gotten ？

    连读，0.8x 也完全听不出来

    你现在收集了多少数据？

  3. I have got all my data, so I am starting to summarize it now, preparing graphs and stuff.

    got all, graphs and 连读听不出来

  1. So think about what you would need to include if you were going to explain your research project to someone with general or casual knowledge about the subject, like you parents.

    太快，听不清

  2. That’ s usually my rule of form, with my parents understand it.

    That’ s usually my rule of thumb, would my parents understand it.

    这是我的经验法则：我的父母会理解这些吗？

    rule of thumb 不知道是什么，would 发音不熟悉

  3. You should use them to indicate what’s your evolution in thought was through time.

    你可以用这些批注来标示随着时间的推进你在思索中的进展。

    in thought 太快听不清，也可以说是因为对这个词不够熟悉

  4. So, just set up, you know, what was the purpose of what you were doing—to try to understand the climate variability of this area—and what you did, and what your approach was.

    approach 不太熟，听不出来

  5. OK. So, for example, I studied meteorological records.

    meteorological 生词，听不出来

  6. The statistical tests are especially important.

    tests are 连读听不出来

  7. I carried a little pad with me all the time and jot down questions or ideas that I don’t want to forget.

    jot down 不知道是什么

  8. For example, I went to the doctor with my daughter and her baby son last week and we knew we wouldn’t remember everything we wanted to ask the doctor, so we actually made a list of five things we wanted answers to.

    太快，听不清也记不住，另外对 made a list of 不熟悉所以更加听不出来

  9. Something so busy now at the end of the semester, I’m getting pretty forgetful these days.

    Since I’m so busy now at the end of the semester, I’m getting pretty forgetful these days.

    对 Since 不够熟悉

  10. Sounds good. I’d be happy to look over a draft first before you hand in the final copy, if you wish.

    Sounds good. I’d be happy to look over a draft version before you hand in the final copy, if you wish.

    对 version 不熟悉，而且两个 ve 音连在一起听不太出来

  11. Great. I’ll plan to get you a draft of the paper by next Friday.

    太快，有连读听不清

总结

###问题

  1. 连读
  2. 太快，听不清，记不住
  3. 不熟悉的词汇

解决办法

  1. 还不知道
  2. 多练
  3. 总结记录下来
