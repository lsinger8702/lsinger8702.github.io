听力训练（初期）Day 2

Posted on 2018-07-14 Edited on 2018-07-15 In TOEFL

TPO 1 Conversation 2

  1. You’ve been observing Mr. Grable’s third-grade class for your approaches to education paper, right?

    你一直将观察 Grable 老师的三年级课堂作为你的毕业论文的突破口，是吧？

    对 observing 不熟悉，所以太快就听不清

  1. It seems that Mr. Grable has mastered the interdisciplinary approach to teaching — the way we’ve been talking about in class.

    看似 Grable 老师已经掌握了我们课堂讨论过的跨学科教学法。

    不知道 interdisciplinary ，后面太快听不清

  2. Oh! OK, yeah, so like when he was teaching them astronomy, he didn’t just teach them the names of the planets, he used the way to teach mythology.

    Oh! OK, yeah, so like when he was teaching them astronomy, he didn’t just teach them the names of the planets, he used it as a way to teach mythology.

    不知道 mythology ，太快听不出来 it as a

  3. Really! So, how did you?

    Really! So, how did he do that?

    太快了，真的完全听不出来

  4. Like, you know, how like Jupiter’s the biggest planet, right, and how Jupiter was the name of the king of the gods in Roman mythology, right?

    太快，biggest 完全听不出来

  5. And each student chose a planet and then did research on it to write a report and make a presentation.

    太快听不出来

总结

问题

  1. 连读
  2. 太快，听不清
  3. 不熟悉的词汇

解决办法

  1. 还不知道
  2. 多练
  3. 总结记录下来
  4. 还是和昨天一样的问题，先多练一下，然后再各个击破
