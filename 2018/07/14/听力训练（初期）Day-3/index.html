听力训练（初期）Day 3

Posted on 2018-07-14 Edited on 2018-07-15 In TOEFL

TPO 3 Conversation 1

  1. Excuse me, I’m supposed to be having my physics class in the science building, but no one’s in the classroom.

    太快没听清

  1. Well, there’s a room assignment sheet on the bulletin board outside this office.

    在咱这办公室外的布告栏上有教室安排告示。

    不认识 bulletin board

  2. You should have got note letter from the registrar’s office about this.

    教务室应该通过信件通知你了的。

    note 太短太快听不清，**registrar’s office **不认识

  3. ‘Cause it says on the computer that the letter was sent out to students a week ago.

    依然没听清 cause

  4. I wonder if I threw it away with all the junk mail by mistake.

    我有可能不小心将它同垃圾邮件一块清除了。

    threw it away 不熟悉

  5. If I’d known this could happen, I would have taken it last semester.

    If I’d known this was going to happen, I would have taken it last semester.

    太快了完全听不出来

  6. Do you have any courses you’re going to take next semester?

    太快了听不清

  7. I just hope it won’t be cancelled again.

    just hope 似乎连读了，听不出来

  8. Give the physics department a call a couple of weeks before the semester starts.

    对 a couple of 不熟悉

  9. They will be able to tell you if they’re planning to go through with it.

    他们能够告诉你他们是否开课。

    太快听不清，感觉小站托福的录音不太清晰，老师的话特别快特别模糊，以后还是用超能学吧

  10. Oh, why don’t you to go change a mail address now.

    不熟悉，所以听不出来

  11. It only takes a minute.

    连读了

TPO 4 Conversation 2

  1. Well, since it’s your class, I guess it’s OK.

    就是，这是你的课，我想没事的。

    对 since 还是不够熟悉，快了就听不清

  2. Ah, yes, do next week.

    Ah, yes, due next week.

    对 due 不熟悉

  3. It’s about, ur, United States Supreme Court Decisions.

    是关于美国最高法院决议

    不认识 Supreme Court

  4. We are looking at the impact of recent cases on property rights, municipal land use cases, owning disputes.

    我们正在研究最近的案子对美国产权，城市用地法案，所有权冲突的影响。

    好几个词都是不认识的，land use 连读了

  5. They are just sitting back, not really doing their fair share of the work and waiting for an A.

    他们只是袖手旁观，没有完成他们分内的工作，却妄想着要拿到A。

    不知道什么是 fair share

  6. It’s kind of stressing me out, because we are getting close to the deadline and I feel like I’m doing everything for this project.

    不熟悉，太快

  7. Ah, the good old free rider problem.

    啊，又是这种搭便车的问题。

    没听过这种说法

  8.  Ah, it’s just a term that describes this situation, when people in the group seek to get the benefits of being in a group without contributing to the work.

    不熟悉，听不出来

  9. Anyway, what exactly do you mean when you say they just sit back?

    不管怎样。你说他们袖手旁观是怎样的情况？

  10. I mean, they’ve been filing following the weekly progress reports with me.

    因为他们一直都有完成每周的进度报告。

    完全听不出 filling，对这种操作不了解，太快听不出来具体的单词

  11. I went off to the stack and found some really good material for my part, but when I got back to our table, they were just goofing off and talking.

    我在书架上找到对我负责的部分很好的资料，但当我回去的时候，他们却在偷懒聊天。

    went off ，goofing off 不认识，stack 的说法也不了解

  12. I know, but I didn’t want to risk the project going down the drain.

    学生：我知道，但是那样的话项目可能流于无形，我可不想冒险。

    risk 不熟悉 going down the drain 的说法不了解

  13. Maybe Teresa and Kevin aren’t that excited about the topic?

    无法在句子里分辨出人名，太快听不出来

  14. And since you picked it, have you thought…talk to them at all about picking a different topic?

    pick 的用法不熟悉，thought 完全听不出来

  15. We don’t have time to start from scratch.

    我们没有时间从头再来。

    不认识 start from scratch

  16. OK, I will let you go ‘cause I know you are so busy.

    好，我会放宽期限因为我知道你很忙，

    太快，依然没听清 ‘cause

总结

问题

  1. 太快听不清楚
  2. 有些特定情境下常用的词汇，或者口语常用的说法不熟悉
  3. 连读

解决办法

  1. 多练
  2. 总结记录下来，看下听力特定场景词汇总结
  3. 多练
