听力训练（初期）Day 4

Posted on 2018-07-15 | In TOEFL | Visitors:

TPO 9 Conversation 1

  1. Yeah, it seems like a great industry to get involved with.

    是的，似乎这是一个可以投入的产业。

    快，不熟，听不清

  1. I mean with a four-year degree in food science, I’ll always be able to find a job.

    快，不熟，听不清

  2. Before entering academia, I worked as a scientist for several food manufacturers and for the US Food and Drug Administration.

    在进入学术研究行列前，我在几个食物制造公司担任过科学家，也曾在美国食物药物管理局任职。

    academia 不认识，但是猜出来了；drug 听不出来

  3. I even worked on a commercial fishing boat in Alaska a couple of summers while I was an undergraduate.

    当我还是大学生的时候，我还连续几个暑假在阿拉斯加某一商用捕鱼船上实习过。

    想不到会出现 fishing 这种词，不太熟悉，地名不清楚

  4. We’d bring in the day’s catch to a floating processor boat where the fish got cleaned, packaged and frozen right at sea.

    我们把当天捕获的鱼带到漂浮加工船，当船还在海上的时候我们会将鱼清洗干净包装并冷冻起来。

    不知道 right at sea 这个表达

  5. As a matter of fact, I’m thought interested in food packaging.

    As a matter of fact, I’m sort of interested in food packaging.

    不熟悉 sort of 的表达

  6. For a long time, I was leaning towards getting my degree in engineering.

    在获得工程学学位之前，我曾经学习了很长一段时间。

    不知道 leaning towards

  7. Well, then you shouldn’t have a problem.

    听不出 n’t，problem 居然完全听不出来

  8. Elsewhere, you might have to hammer courses together on your own.

    要是在别处，你可能就要自己扛下了。

    Elsewhere， hammer courses 不认识

  9. I guess I luck out then.

    不认识 luck out

  10. I am… so since my appointment today is to discuss my term paper topic, I wanted to ask, could I write about food packaging?

    那么，由于我今天跟您约是想讨论我的学期论文话题，我本来是想问，我可以写关于食物包装的话题吗？

    听不出来 so since ，不太熟悉这种表达

  11. I realize we’re supposed to research foodborne bacteria, but food packaging must play a role in all of that, right?

    反正就是听不出来，foodborne bacteria 不认识

  12. Absolutely! Maybe you should do some preliminary research on that.

    当然了。也许你应该做些初期研究工作。

    preliminary 不认识

  13. I’m overwhelmed.

    内容太多了我看不过来。

    不熟悉，而且连读了

  14. You know, clear plastic bottles, opaque bottles, carton board containers..

    你知道，透光的塑料瓶，不透光的牛奶瓶，硬纸盒等等。

    opaque bottles, carton board 不认识

  15. Yeah, and I write a study that showed how like can give milk a funny flavor and decrease its nutritional value.

    Yeah, and I read a study that showed how light can give milk a funny flavor and decrease its nutritional value.

    学生：是的，我读过一篇文章，说光线能够让牛奶发出异味，降低营养价值。

    分不清 write 和 过去式的 read 的读音，light 没想到会出现

  16. What’s up about that?

    这是怎么回事呢？

    ”up about“ 连读

  17. That might be one reason that opaque bottles haven’t really caught on.

    这可能是为射门不透光的牛奶瓶没有被使用的原因之一。

    caught on 不认识，真的听不出 n’t 啊

  18. You shouldn’t base your paper on only one study.

    你的论文不该只基于一方面的研究。

    真的听不出 n’t 啊，base 不太熟悉这种表达

  19. Find out if there is any scientific reasons they aren’t used more widely?

    关于这些瓶子没有得到广泛应用的问题，找出是否有什么科学的原因。

    真的听不出 n’t 啊

  20. Maybe opaque bottles aren’t as good at keeping bacteria from growing in milk after the bottle has been opened for something.

    也许在牛奶瓶开盖了以后，不透光的牛奶瓶就不像封闭时那样能有效防止细菌滋长吧。

    真的听不出 n’t 啊

  21. You know, there is a dairy not far from here in Chelsea.

    你知道，在切尔西市，离这里不远的地方有一个奶牛厂。

    地名听不出，dairy 不认识

总结

问题

  1. 太快听不清楚
  2. 有些特定情境下常用的词汇，或者口语常用的说法不熟悉
  3. 地名不熟悉
  4. 连读
  5. 听不出 n’t

解决办法

  1. 多练
  2. 总结记录下来，看下听力特定场景词汇总结
  3. 学会分辨出句子里人名和地名
  4. 多练
  5. 还不知道
